Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

RightOnPar LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at getrightonpar.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our site or contact us, how we use it, and how we protect it.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information we collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide to us. This includes:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number when you submit our project intake form
  • A description of your project or idea
  • Any files or images you choose to upload through the contact form
  • Your estimated budget range if you choose to provide it

We do not collect any information automatically, run analytics, place tracking cookies, or use any third-party advertising on this website.

How we use your information

Any information you submit through our contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your project inquiry
  • Discuss your project requirements with you
  • Contact you about your submission if needed

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

How your form submissions are processed

Our contact form is powered by Formspree, a third-party form processing service. When you submit the form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers and then forwarded to our email inbox. Formspree's own privacy policy applies to the handling of your data during transmission. You can review it at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Data retention

We retain your submitted information only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for any follow-up related to a potential project engagement. If you would like us to delete your information at any time, contact us at drew@getrightonpar.com and we will do so promptly.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies. We do not track your behavior, run analytics, or place any tracking technology on your device.

Third-party links

Our website contains links to external sites (our products hosted on Vercel, and other third-party services). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

Children's privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
